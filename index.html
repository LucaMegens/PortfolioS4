Personal project: Generative art

In this personal project I wanted to persue a part of my
Passion Project from Semester 3 Media Design.

I called this Passion Project "The Story of Life". The goal of my passion project was to tell a story about life, death and what it means to live. I wanted to achieve this by using abstract motion graphics, kinetic typography and generative art in a sort of webapp where the user could discover the story by solving simple abstract puzzles.

The part that I wanted to further persue was the generative art part, since I didn't have the time in my passion project to do this. I spoke to Judith about this and she though that this was a nice idea, she gave me a tip to try to fully work out the immersiveness of a small part of my Passion Project. I also asked Geert-Jan some advice about generative art, he gave me the tip to try and learn the coding language "Processing".

Duo Project: Orbs of Depression

For this duo project I wanted to learn even more about generative art and because of that I got paired up with Edin Banda, because he also had interests in generative art.

The project is called "Orbs of Depression". The goal of our project was to give insight of the positive and negative emotions a person that suffers from depression experiences.
We wanted to achieve this by telling a story about those emotions using two 3d abstract orbs that each represent an emotion.

Group Project: Create a synthetic voice

This group project was commisioned by Greenhouse. We got the assignment from Greenhouse to design and develop a voice for the virtual influencer Nova Green. We have to look into multiple Text To Speech (TTS) libraries and systems but also look at how the voice should sound. Greenhouse would appreciate it if we could sync the voice with Nova Green's face.

We have divided this assignment in two tasks

Designing a voice for Nova Green; we will use multiple tools to get some voices we could finetune for synthesizing the voice.

Building the voice for Nova Green; we will use some sort of library like wavenet/waveglow to synthesize the voice so we can finetune its pitch and we can sync the voice to Nova Green's face eventually.
